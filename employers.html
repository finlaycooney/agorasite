Frequently Asked Questions

How does your vetting process work for Web3 talent?

+

Our vetting process includes multiple stages: technical assessments specific to blockchain technologies, portfolio reviews, reference checks, and culture fit evaluations. For technical roles, we conduct code reviews and challenge-based assessments. We verify experience with specific protocols and knowledge of cryptographic principles for specialized positions.

What makes Agora4 different from other tech recruiters?

+

Unlike general tech recruiters, we exclusively focus on Web3, blockchain, and crypto talent. Our team includes former blockchain developers and project managers who understand the technology deeply. We maintain active participation in major Web3 communities and events, giving us access to passive candidates not available on traditional job boards.

How long does it typically take to fill a position?

+

For most technical Web3 roles, we can present qualified candidates within 1-2 days. The entire process from initial consultation to offer acceptance typically takes 3-4 weeks, though this varies based on role complexity and your interview process. For specialized positions like cryptography researchers or protocol architects, the timeline may extend to 4-6 weeks. We will work to deadlines that you need.

Do you work with early-stage projects or just established companies?

+

We work with Web3 projects at all stages, from pre-seed startups to established protocols. For early-stage projects, we offer flexible payment terms and can help with technical co-founder matching. We understand the unique challenges of building foundational teams in the blockchain space and provide guidance on competitive compensation packages to attract top talent in this competitive market.
